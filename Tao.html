Huang Zitao

Qingdao

While in Exo, Tao was also the maknae. Tao is known for his effortless rapping skills in the Mandarin songs. Tao has a very interesting and unique character. While he may look scary and intimidating while showcasing his martial arts skills, he has a really cute and confident personality.
Tao left Exo because he had many injuries. Tao has been pursuing a solo career that has been extremely successful. Every song Tao releases always ends up on the charts. Tao has also been acting more. Tao's goal is to make Cpop known around the world. He is not afraid to be his confident self. Tao's music is produced by himself and a close friend. Tao oversees everything when producing music and writing lyrics.
