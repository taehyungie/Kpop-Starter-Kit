Lu Han

April 20, 1990
Beijing

Luhan was discovered while studying abroad at Yonsei Unversity, which is one of the most prestigious universities in South Korea. Luhan was also one of the oldest memebers. Luhan was a vocalist in the Mandarin version of the songs. Luhan usually sang alongside Chen, Baekhyun, and D.O.. He was also one of the oldest members.
Luhan left Exo because of unfair treatment. Luhan stated that the Chinese members are treated differently in comparison to the Korean members. About a year after his departure, he released his first album, Reloaded. Djemba Djemba, a producer famous for working with artists such as Justin Bieber, helped Luhan produce the album. The album's sound was very different to his usual Exo songs, so he was a bit hesitant at first. Luhan experimented with different genres of music such as trap. Luhan has also released great ballads. He has appeared on many reality shows and movies.
