Kris

吴亦凡

"You're young, be adventurous, don't give up, and then your dream will come true."

Wu Kris
November 6, 1990
Guangzhou

Kris was the main rapper in the Mandarin versions of the Exo songs. Kris is fluent in English; he has lived in Canada before. Before joining Exo, Kris wanted to play basketball professionally.
Kris left Exo because of injuries, as well. Kris has been in many successful movies and shows. Recently, Kris has played for Team Canada at the NBA All Stars Game.
