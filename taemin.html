Lee Taemin
July 18, 1993

Taemin is SHINee's maknae. He debuted when he was 15. Taemin is a well known dancer; one of S.M. Entertainment's best dancers. He started dancing in 3rd grade. He has released two successful albums with very creative music videos and amazing choreography. Taemin is strong and fierce on stage, but shy and cute offstage. When he was a child, Taemin dreamed of protecting the earth.
