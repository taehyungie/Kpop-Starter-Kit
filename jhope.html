Jung Hoseok
February 18, 1994
Gwangju

Jhope is your hope and your angel. He is literal sunshine. Jhope is BTS's happy virus who spreads happiness to the members around him. His laugh and smile are infectuous. Jhope is also an amazing dancer; before joining BTS, he was part of a dance team. He is not afraid to be himself. Jhope is also a rapper who used to be insecure of his rapping abilities, but with practice, he has improved tremendously. He has even released a song "1 VERSE" which showcases his talent.
