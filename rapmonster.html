Rap Monster

랩몬스터

"I can eat any kind of beat like a monster."

Kim Namjoon
12 September 1994
Seoul

Rap Monster is BTS's beloved leader and main rapper. When he's not rapping, he's taking care of the other members, writing lyrics, or producing. While Rap Monster is great at rapping, he is not a "dance monster." Even if his dancing isn't the greatest, he's still pretty good. Before Rap Monster even debuted with BTS, Rap Monster was an amazing student, scoring high on exams and being in the nation's 1%. Now, he's charting high on the music charts with BTS. Rap Monster works very hard and raps with passion. He even has his own mixtape that reflects upon how broken society is.

Nicknames: Rap Mon, Leader Mon, God of Destruction
