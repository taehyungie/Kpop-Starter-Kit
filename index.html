Bangtan Boys

방탄소년단

BTS first started on June 13, 2013. BTS is known for their innovative dance moves, hip hop, pop, and R&B music. They are not only very famous in South Korea, but around the world! Fuse and Billboard have given Bangtan Boys very positive critical acclaim, especially with their latest albums, The Most Beautiful Moment in Life Pt. 1, The Most Beautiful Moment in Life Pt. 2, and The Most Beautiful Moment in Life: Young Forever. Their world tour in 2015, the Red Bullet Tour, attracted many fans around the world. Many people consider them to be some of the best dancers in kpop. The members are not afraid to be themselves. They are also very interactive with fans!

Exo

엑소

Exo has been around since 2011. They sing in Korean and Chinese. Originally, six members sang in Korean (Exo-K), while the other six members sang in Chinese (Exo-M). Those two subgroups used to constitute Exo. Right now, Exo-K and Exo-M are combined; they all sing songs in Korean and Chinese. Exo also has amazing dancers and vocalists. Exo became very popular after their "XOXO" album (their best selling album) which includes the hit "Wolf." Exo breaks their own records every time they release a new album. They always sell millions of albums.

What is Kpop?

Kpop is South Korean pop music. Kpop is widely recognized for the complex dance routines and hard working pop stars. One of the most famous songs was published in 2012, “GANGNAM STYLE” by PSY. It is still the most viewed music video on Youtube.
Kpop is a way to unite people from different cultures and backgrounds. Even if you don’t speak Korean you can still have fun!

Kpop Culture

Kpop fans are very unique. We are loyal, loving, and very creative. Twitter, Tumblr, and Facebook are the main platforms in which Kpop Fans can communicate. Many clips, memes, and photos are circulated online in the Kpop community. At concerts, people have cardboard cutouts of their favorite member or even make light up signs with their favorite member's name. Fans either recite lyrics during pauses in songs or shout words of encouragement. These fan chats are memorized and can be sung at concerts or when casually listening to a song.
